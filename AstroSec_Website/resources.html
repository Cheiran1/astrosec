The Science Behind the Story

NASA Data & Science Sources

Saniya (Resource Analyst) ensured the factual basis of our story. We leveraged data and insights from the following NASA programs:

  • Heliophysics Missions (SOHO, STEREO): Used real-world visualizations and data to accurately model flares and CMEs.
  • Space Radiation and Astronaut Safety: Consulted NASA's public resources regarding the impact of solar events on ISS crew and shielding requirements.
  • Space Weather Community Impacts: Referenced NASA and NOAA materials detailing effects on power grids, satellite operations, and high-latitude aviation (pilots).

Technologies & Tools Used

Our multi-disciplinary team utilized the following stack for rapid development and delivery:

  • Development & Deployment (Cherian & Kurian): HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript (for story interactivity/navigation). Deployed via GitHub Pages.
  • Content Creation (Parvathy & Pranav): Google Docs/Slides for storyboarding, and simple image editing tools for children-friendly visuals.
  • Visuals: Canva/simple illustration tools were used to create high-contrast, appealing illustrations.
