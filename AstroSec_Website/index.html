Stellar Stories: Astra's Space Weather Adventure

2025 NASA Space Apps Challenge Entry by Team AstroSec

We transformed complex solar physics into an engaging digital children's story narrated by Astronaut Astra. Our project simplifies space weather phenomena, like solar flares and CMEs, and vividly details their varying, real-world impact on life in orbit and on Earth.
